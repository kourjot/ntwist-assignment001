Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

What services does NTWIST provide?

NTWIST provides innovative software solutions for industrial optimization and data analytics. Our products help companies in mining, energy, and manufacturing to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance sustainability through advanced AI and machine learning technologies.

How do I get started with NTWIST software?

Getting started with NTWIST is simple. Contact our sales team through the form on our website, and we'll schedule a consultation to understand your needs. Our team will then provide a customized demonstration and implementation plan tailored to your specific requirements.

What industries do you serve?

NTWIST primarily serves the following industries:

  • Mining and minerals processing
  • Oil and gas
  • Energy and utilities
  • Manufacturing
  • Chemical processing

Our solutions are adaptable and can be customized for various industrial applications.

What technical support options are available?

NTWIST offers comprehensive technical support through multiple channels:

  • 24/7 emergency support line
  • Online ticket system
  • Knowledge base and documentation
  • Regular maintenance and updates
  • Dedicated account managers for enterprise clients

Our support team consists of experienced engineers who understand both the software and industry-specific challenges.

Can NTWIST software integrate with our existing systems?

Yes, NTWIST software is designed with integration in mind. Our solutions can connect with most major industrial control systems, SCADA systems, data historians, and enterprise resource planning (ERP) software. We use standard protocols and APIs to ensure seamless data flow between systems, minimizing disruption to your existing operations.

What kind of ROI can we expect from implementing NTWIST solutions?

Clients typically see ROI within 6-12 months of implementing NTWIST solutions. Our optimization software has delivered:

  • 3-10% increase in throughput
  • 5-15% reduction in energy consumption
  • Significant decrease in process variability
  • Enhanced recovery rates for valuable materials
  • Reduced downtime through predictive maintenance

We work closely with each client to track and measure specific KPIs relevant to their operations.
